Hello, there!

My name is Scott and I am a Full Stack Developer.

After spending 18 years in the beer industry, I was unfortunately made redundant during the pandemic...twice!

Shaken but determined, I retrained as a Software Developer, completing a 12-month online Coding Traineeship, where I was introduced to the basics of coding and full-stack development.

During the course, I became proficient in a wide range of front-end and server-side programming languages, and gained a practical understanding of various libraries, frameworks, platforms and technologies.

As a newly-qualified Developer, I began to build my portfolio through the creation of a diverse and comprehensive range of responsive, accessible and visually-appealing apps and websites.

I am a bright, highly-motivated individual who loves a challenge, and who is currently on the lookout for entry-level opportunities within the coding and software development industry.

I qualified as a Full Stack Developer through the completion of an online Coding Traineeship. This course began by teaching the basic fundamentals of essential front-end coding languages: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, before moving on to more complex and challenging subjects.

In Stage 2, I delved deeper in more specific front and back-end languages and technologies. During this stage, I completed modules and received certification in Full Stack Engineering, jQuery, Python 3, PHP, Java and C#.

At the end of the course, I sat my Microsoft 98-383: Introduction to Programming Using HTML and CSS examination, becoming fully-certified as a Microsoft Technology Associate.

Since graduating, I have continued to study independently, gaining further certifications in the Sass and Vue.js frameworks.
